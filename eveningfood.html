EVENING FOOD

An exciting stroll through our beautiful city filled with delicious food and drinks that locals crave for in the late afternoon/early evening.

But what do locals eat in the evening? If mashed potatoes and meat stews is your answer, then pat yourself on the back. You've done your research. But there's more. In Fremantle you can virtually find any kind of cuisine you can think of. Lebanese, dim sum of every shape, size, and filling, even Poke bowls! But what kind of street food do enjoy on a more regular basis? Where do we hang out after dark? We know you're curious, so our Evening Experience is the answer to those burning questions.

Instead of perching yourself at a restaurant all night, why not join us to enjoy a moveable feast? Let us take you out to town like old friends, to places where we eat and drink, places old and new, to give you a feel and taste of our city that you can't really find in guidebooks.

This is 3.5 hours of an exciting stroll through our beautiful city filled with delicious foods and drinks that locals crave for in the late afternoon and early evening. We hit up several places to enjoy different dishes and lovely stories of our partners and their businesses. And yes, there will be some alcohol too! We'll make you feel like a local straightaway!

TOUR DETAILS

• Tours start at 6:00 pm – This is a food tasting tour. Plan to arrive early at meeting point for the tour and ready to indulge in the best food Fremantle can offer.

• Tour Duration – The tour is around 3.5 hours. The chosen restaurants are able to accommodate for those with wheelchairs, service animals and specific dietary requirements.

• Professional Guides – You will be led by a professional guide who is an expert on culinary skills and delights.

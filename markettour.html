MARKET TOUR

Explore and support the local traders and musicians down at the markets that will immerse you in the culture of Fremantle.

BOOK NOW

$35

One of the most visited parts of Fremantle, the markets have been popular amongst locals and tourists since it was built in 1897. The markets aren't just full of history but also home to many, many stores ranging from clothing retailers to fresh produce.

During this tour, Flipside will take you on a stroll through the markets to visit all these amazing stores and their staff who have resided in the Fremantle markets for decades, being passed down through generations. Not only will you find out many stories about Fremantle along the way chatting to the store staff but you will be able to sample and purchase items as you go and listen to the beautiful music of the local musicians.

TOUR DETAILS

• Tours start at 12:00 pm – This tour is a walking tour around the Fremantle Markets. There will be plenty of opportunities to purchase goods from the stores so please ensure that you are prepared with cash on hand, as card payments are accepted at most stores but not all.

• Tour Duration – The tour is around 3 hours.

• Professional Guides – You will be led by a professional guide who is an expert on the history of Fremantle Markets.

Market Tours $35 (includes samples of fresh produce and goods)
